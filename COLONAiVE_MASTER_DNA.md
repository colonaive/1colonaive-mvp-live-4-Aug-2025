# Project COLONAiVE™ — Master Strategic Doctrine

## 1. Core Mission
The primary mission of Project COLONAiVE™ is to increase colorectal cancer (CRC) screening uptake at a population level.

Low screening uptake is the fundamental global problem:
- Leads to fewer colonoscopies
- Causes delayed diagnosis
- Results in preventable deaths

This is the central problem we solve.

---

## 2. Strategic Objective
All activities must drive:

- Increased screening uptake
- Earlier triage to colonoscopy
- Earlier detection
- Reduced CRC mortality

---

## 3. Clinical Positioning Framework

- Colonoscopy = Gold standard (diagnosis + early treatment)
- Blood-based screening (e.g. ColonAiQ®) = High-sensitivity triage tool

Messaging must always follow:

Screening → Risk identification → Colonoscopy → Early intervention

---

## 4. Messaging Doctrine (CRITICAL)

All communications must subtly reinforce:

- Low screening uptake is the problem
- Screening saves lives
- Modern screening options exist
- A national movement is underway

Never:
- Oversell a single product
- Undermine colonoscopy
- Create conflict with existing screening methods
