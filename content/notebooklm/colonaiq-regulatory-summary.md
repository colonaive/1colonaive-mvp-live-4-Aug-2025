# ColonAiQ Regulatory Status Summary

## Current Marker Panel
The aligned commercial configuration is based on six methylation markers: SEPTIN9, SEPTIN9 region 2, BCAT1, IKZF1, BCAN, and VAV3.

## Regulatory Alignment
Singapore currently has HSA registration for the five-marker version, with an update to the six-marker version planned. The six-marker configuration is aligned with the CE-marked version intended for Europe and the planned CDSCO pathway in India. In China, the product holds NMPA approval under certificate number 20243400902, valid until 13 May 2029.

## Strategic Consideration
The regulatory objective is to align the final commercial configuration across major jurisdictions wherever possible. A more consistent panel would support a single technical dossier, a more coherent clinical evidence package, simpler post-market surveillance, and easier expansion into additional ASEAN markets.

## IFU Guardrails
ColonAiQ is a screening assay rather than a diagnostic test. Positive results require confirmation with colonoscopy, and negative results do not exclude colorectal cancer.
