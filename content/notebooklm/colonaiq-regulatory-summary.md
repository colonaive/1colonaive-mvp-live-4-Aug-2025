# ColonAiQ — Regulatory Status Summary

6 methylation markers: SEPTIN9, SEPTIN9 region 2, BCAT1, IKZF1, BCAN, VAV3

## Regulatory Alignment

| Jurisdiction | Classification | Status |

## Strategy
Align final commercial configuration across all major jurisdictions to reduce regulatory fragmentation. Benefits: single technical dossier, consistent clinical evidence package, simplified post-market surveillance, easier ASEAN expansion.

## IFU Guardrails
ColonAiQ is a screening test. Must be confirmed with colonoscopy. Positive ≠ diagnosis. Negative ≠ rule-out.
