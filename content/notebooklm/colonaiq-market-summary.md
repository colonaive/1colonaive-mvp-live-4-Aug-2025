# ColonAiQ Market Strategy Summary

## Geographic Focus
The current priority markets are Singapore, the Philippines, and India. Each market has different regulatory and commercial requirements, but the overall expansion strategy follows the same logic: secure regulatory clearance, build clinical confidence, establish delivery infrastructure, and then scale referral pathways.

## Market Entry Approach
Market development is expected to move in sequence from regulatory approval to key opinion leader validation, hospital adoption, laboratory partnerships, and physician referral activation. That sequence is intended to reduce execution risk and support a clinically credible launch.

## Core Value Proposition
ColonAiQ is positioned as a non-invasive blood-based screening option that may appeal to people who are unwilling to start with stool testing or colonoscopy. The practical value proposition is higher participation, more efficient triage toward colonoscopy, and a greater chance of detecting disease before late-stage presentation.

## Key Stakeholders
The most important stakeholders are colorectal surgeons, gastroenterologists, diagnostic laboratories, hospital systems, and public-sector screening stakeholders. Their support determines clinical adoption, workflow integration, and long-term population relevance.
