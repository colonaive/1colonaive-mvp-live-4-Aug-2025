# ColonAiQ — Market Strategy Summary

## Target Countries
Singapore, Philippines, India

## Market Entry Sequence
1. Regulatory clearance
2. KOL validation
3. Hospital adoption
4. Lab partnerships
5. Physician referral networks

## Core Value Proposition
- Non-invasive screening (blood test vs stool or colonoscopy)
- Higher patient compliance
- Efficient triage for colonoscopy
- Reduces late-stage diagnosis

## Key Stakeholders
- Colorectal surgeons
- Gastroenterologists
- Diagnostic laboratories
- Hospital systems
- Government screening programs
