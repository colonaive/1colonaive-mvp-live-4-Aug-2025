CRC NEWS INTELLIGENCE WORKFLOW

Purpose:
Monitor global colorectal cancer screening news and convert important findings into awareness and educational content.

Sources:

• PubMed
• Medical journals
• RSS feeds
• Google News search
• Public health announcements
• CRC screening campaigns


PROCESS

1. detect new CRC-related research articles
2. evaluate relevance to colorectal cancer screening
3. classify article importance
4. generate plain-language summary
5. generate marketing awareness content


OUTPUT TYPES

Research summary

LinkedIn awareness post

Clinic email bulletin

Patient-friendly educational snippet


CONTENT RULES

All generated content must:

• remain scientifically accurate
• avoid unverified claims
• avoid diagnostic claims
• encourage screening and colonoscopy follow-up


COCKPIT INTEGRATION

Important articles should be flagged for the CEO Cockpit CRC Intelligence panel.

Suggested actions may include:

• LinkedIn post generation
• clinic bulletin distribution
• website educational updates
