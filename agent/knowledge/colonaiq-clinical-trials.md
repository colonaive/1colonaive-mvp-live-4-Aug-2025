# ColonAiQ Clinical Trial and Validation Strategy

## Singapore Clinical Validation Program

A key component of ColonAiQ adoption strategy in Singapore is the development of **independent local clinical validation studies**.

The objective is to generate Singapore-specific evidence supporting clinical use of the assay in colorectal cancer screening and early detection.

---

# Khoo Teck Puat Hospital (KTPH) Investigator Study

Proposed study lead:

Dr Daniel Lee  
Chief of Colorectal and Geriatric Surgical Service  
Khoo Teck Puat Hospital

Study concept:

ColonAiQ testing will be performed on patients undergoing colonoscopy.

Study objectives:

• evaluate concordance between ColonAiQ results and colonoscopy findings  
• validate test performance in Singapore population  
• generate real-world evidence for clinical adoption

Funding model:

Potential support from institutional research budgets and external grant funding.

# Strategic Value of Clinical Trials

Local clinical validation provides several advantages:

1. Builds trust among clinicians  
2. Generates region-specific performance data  
3. Supports regulatory updates and dossier amendments  
4. Strengthens payer and government discussions

Clinical validation programs therefore form a core component of the ColonAiQ go-to-market strategy.
