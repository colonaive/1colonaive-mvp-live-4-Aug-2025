ColonAiVE Platform Strategy

ColonAiVE is a public colorectal cancer awareness and screening platform.

Objectives:

1 increase screening uptake
2 educate public about CRC risk
3 connect patients with screening providers
4 support early detection initiatives

Technology stack:

Vite
React
Supabase
Netlify

Core features:

• clinic directory
• educational resources
• screening awareness campaigns
• regional market pages
