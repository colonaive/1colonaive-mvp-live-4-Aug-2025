# ColonAiQ Marketing Guidelines

## Core Message

Get screened.
Get scoped.
Save your life.

ColonAiQ is a blood-based screening test designed to improve colorectal cancer screening compliance.

## Regulatory Constraints (Singapore HSA)

ColonAiQ is a screening tool, not a diagnostic test.

Positive results must be confirmed by colonoscopy.

Negative results do not rule out colorectal cancer.

Marketing materials must not claim diagnostic capability.

## Audience Types

1. Doctors and Clinics
2. Patients and Public

Each requires different communication style.

## Doctor-Facing Materials

Focus on:

• clinical evidence  
• biomarker panel  
• workflow integration  
• patient compliance improvement

## Patient-Facing Materials

Focus on:

• convenience
• early detection
• screening importance
• colonoscopy follow-up

Avoid technical claims beyond screening context.
