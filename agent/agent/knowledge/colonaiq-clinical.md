ColonAiQ Clinical Context

Colorectal cancer progression:

adenoma → dysplasia → carcinoma

Progression time: typically 10–15 years.

Early detection dramatically improves survival.

Stage I survival:
>90%

Stage IV survival:
~12%

Blood-based screening improves compliance compared with stool tests and colonoscopy.

Clinical workflow:

1 blood screening
2 positive patients referred for colonoscopy
3 colonoscopy confirms diagnosis
4 pathology determines stage
