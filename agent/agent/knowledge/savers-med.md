Saver's Med focuses on the commercialization and regional expansion of advanced cancer screening technologies.

Current flagship technology:

ColonAiQ

A blood-based ctDNA methylation screening assay for colorectal cancer.

Core markets targeted:

Singapore  
Philippines  
India

Future regional expansion potential includes broader Southeast Asia.
